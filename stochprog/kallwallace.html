Kall and Wallace text available

A slightly modified second edition is available via Stein Wallace's webpage, at http://home.himolde.no/~wallace/manujw.pdf

The following is an email from Stein Wallace, on Feb 19, 2003:

Colleagues,

Peter Kall and I are happy to announce that you can now download our stochastic programming textbook from 1994 for free. Wiley has now released the copyright on the book, and we wish to make the text available to the scientific community.

We hope that you take the opportunity to use the book for yourself and your students.

You can find the book in pdf-format by visiting my home page (see below) or the home page of Peter Kall - http://www.unizh.ch/ior/Pages/Deutsch/Mitglieder/Kall/Kall.php under bibliography.

Or simply go directly to http://www.unizh.ch/ior/Pages/Deutsch/Mitglieder/Kall/bib/ka-wal-94.pdf
