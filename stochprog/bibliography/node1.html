Schedule

Student presentations will occur on December 11, from 6.30pm to 9.30pm in DCC 239.

Each talk will last approximately fifteen to twenty minutes. The order of presentation will be random.

You should describe the major contribution(s) of the paper you present. You should type up a short (3-5 pages) analysis of the paper(s), which I will photocopy and give out before your presentation. Thus, please give me your summary by December 10.

In order to encourage questions, your grade will not be lowered if you are unable to answer questions from other students, but it may be raised. Moreover, I may give some bonus points for asking a particularly good question.
