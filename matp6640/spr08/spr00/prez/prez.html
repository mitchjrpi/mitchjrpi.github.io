Papers to be presented

You should type up a short (3-5 pages) analysis of the paper(s), which I will photocopy and give out before your presentation. Thus, please give me your summary at least a day before your presentation. Feel free to talk to me if you need help.

In order to encourage questions, your grade will not be lowered if you are unable to answer questions from other students, but it may be raised. Moreover, I may give some bonus points for asking a particularly good question.
