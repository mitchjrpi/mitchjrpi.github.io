Infeasible interior point methods

The best computational methods work towards feasibility and optimality simultaneously. This was originally done with no proof of convergence, but there are now papers which show that such an algorithm converges, and that in fact such algorithms have good convergence properties. Mizuno et al. [36] discuss infeasible potential reduction methods.
