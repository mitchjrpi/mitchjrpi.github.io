Affine methods

These algorithms just involve rescaling. They are easy to describe but hard to analyze. Gonzaga [22] surveys these methods. Mascarenhas [30] and Hall and Vanderbei [24] are concerned with cases where the affine scaling algorithm may not converge to the optimal solution. Tsuchiya [47] analyses convergence of the algorithm for degenerate linear programs. Monteiro and Tsuchiya [37] and Muramatsu and Vanderbei [39] examine affine algorithms for convex quadratic programming problems and semidefinite programming problems, respectively.
