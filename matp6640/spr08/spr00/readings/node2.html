Papers on reserve

In the past, I've placed a large number of papers on reserve. That is not so necessary this year, because of the publication of the books by Wright, by Ye, by Roos, Terlaky and Vial, and by Vanderbei, all of which are on reserve.

I will place a couple of recent papers on reserve and I will have pointers to more papers from the course web page. These include a survey paper by Steve Wright [50] and a paper on the use of homogenization techniques to prove many results about linear programming and interior point methods by Terlaky [45].
