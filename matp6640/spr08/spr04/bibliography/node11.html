Second order cone programming (SOCP)

Muramatsu [31] and Goldfarb [16] describe simplex-like methods for SOCPs. Kim et al. [22] discuss SOCP relaxations of semidefinite programs. Goldfarb and Iyengar [17] give robust formulations of various optimization problems. For a different approach to robustness, see Bertsimas and Sym [7].
