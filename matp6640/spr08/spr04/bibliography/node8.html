Algorithms for SDP

Helmberg et al. [21] discuss a bundle method for solving SDP problems, which uses a technique from nonsmoooth optimization applied to a dual formulation. Muramatsu [30,32] shows that affine algorithms may fail to converge to the optimal solution of a SDP problem. Benson [6] describes a parallel SDP algorithm. Burer and Monteiro [9] discuss convergence issues with a nonlinear programming approach to SDP. Fujisawa et al. [13] describe a method to preprocess semidefinite programs.
