MATP6640/DSES6770 Linear Programming
A Sample Writeup

Attached is a sample writeup, for the Stochastic Programming course. This discusses the paper Variance reduction and objective function evaluation in stochastic linear programs by Julie Higle, in the INFORMS Journal of Computing, Volume 10(2), 1998, pages 236-247. The paper is available online from the library, and the library also has a hardcopy.

The writeup describes the content of the paper, discusses what it tries to contribute, and evaluates the success of that contribution.
