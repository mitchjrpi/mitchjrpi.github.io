Interior point column generation

Goffin and Vial [14] showed that multiple cuts can be added at once while retaining a pseudopolynomial algorithm. Surveys of the theoretical performance of interior point column generation methods include [15,27].

Interior point column generation methods have been used to solve financial optimization problems [18, 19.5], Lagrangian relaxations of integer programs [10], and integer programming problems [26,28], among others.
