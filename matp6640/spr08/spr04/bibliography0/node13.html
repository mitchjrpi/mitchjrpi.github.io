Interior point column generation

Goffin and Vial [13] showed that multiple cuts can be added at once while retaining a pseudopolynomial algorithm. Surveys of the theoretical performance of interior point column generation methods include [14,25].

Interior point column generation methods have been used to solve financial optimization problems [16], Lagrangian relaxations of integer programs [9], and integer programming problems [24,26], among others.
