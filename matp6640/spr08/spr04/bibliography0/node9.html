Algorithms for SDP

Helmberg et al. [19] discuss a bundle method for solving SDP problems, which uses a technique from nonsmoooth optimization applied to a dual formulation. Muramatsu [28,30] shows that affine algorithms may fail to converge to the optimal solution of a SDP problem. Benson [5] describes a parallel SDP algorithm. Burer and Monteiro [8] discuss convergence issues with a nonlinear programming approach to SDP. Fujisawa et al. [12] describe a method to preprocess semidefinite programs.
