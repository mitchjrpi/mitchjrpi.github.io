Second order cone programming (SOCP)

Muramatsu [29] describes a simplex-like method for SOCPs. Kim et al. [20] discuss SOCP relaxations of semidefinite programs. Goldfarb and Iyengar [15] give robust formulations of various optimization problems. For a different approach to robustness, see Bertsimas and Sym [6].
