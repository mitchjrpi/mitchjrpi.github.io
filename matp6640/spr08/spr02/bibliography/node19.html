Nonlinear programming

Interior point methods can be extended to convex programming problems; see, for example, Andersen and Ye [6] Methods for more general nonlinear programs have also been proposed, by Byrd et al. [17] and by Vanderbei and Shanno [57], among others. Wächter and Biegler [58] point out that some care is needed in these extensions, particularly if the step length is determined after the direction.
