Bibliography

The links to journal web pages, namely to SIOPT, Mathematical Programming, SIMAX, and JACM, will only work from on campus.

IPM Online and Optimization Online point to the announcement of the paper on the Interior Point Methods Online website or the Optimization Online website The announcement will contain a link to the text of the paper.

Many more papers can be found in an online bibliography that I maintain. If you find a paper in here (or elsewhere) that you'd like to present, talk to me.
