Affine methods

These algorithms just involve rescaling. They are easy to describe but hard to analyze. Gonzaga [27] surveys these methods. Mascarenhas [36], Hall and Vanderbei [29], and Castillo and Barnes [18] are concerned with cases where the affine scaling algorithm may not converge to the optimal solution. Tsuchiya [56] analyses convergence of the algorithm for degenerate linear programs. Monteiro and Tsuchiya [45] and Muramatsu and Vanderbei [47] examine affine algorithms for convex quadratic programming problems and semidefinite programming problems, respectively.
