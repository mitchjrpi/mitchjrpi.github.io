Nonconvex Quadratically Constrained Quadratic Programs (QCQPs)

Here you can find the data used in the paper Convex Quadratic Relaxations of Nonconvex Quadratically Constrained Quadratic Programs by John Mitchell, Jong-Shi Pang, and Bin Yu.

The data files contain three classes of nonconvex QCQP problems:

  • Problems with diamond shaped feasible regions.
  • Bilevel problems.
  • Randomly generated problems with a more general structure.
